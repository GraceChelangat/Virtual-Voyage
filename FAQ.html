FAQ

What is a virtual tour?

A virtual tour is an online simulation of an existing location, usually composed of a sequence of videos or still images.

How do I book a virtual tour?

You can book a virtual tour through our website by selecting your desired tour package and following the booking instructions.

What technical requirements are needed?

All you need is a computer or mobile device with internet access. For the best experience, we recommend using a VR headset.

Can I use VR headsets with your tours?

Yes, our virtual tours are compatible with most VR headsets, offering an immersive experience.

Are your virtual tours narrated?

Yes, many of our virtual tours include narration to provide detailed information about the locations you're exploring.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes, we offer special discounts for schools and students. Please contact us for more details.
